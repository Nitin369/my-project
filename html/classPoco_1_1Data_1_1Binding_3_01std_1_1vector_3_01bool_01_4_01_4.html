Detailed Description

Specialization for std::vector<bool>. This specialization is necessary due to the nature of std::vector<bool>. For details, see the standard library implementation of std::vector<bool> or S. Meyers: "Effective STL" (Copyright Addison-Wesley 2001), Item 18: "Avoid using vector<bool>."

The workaround employed here is using std::deque<bool> as an internal replacement container.

IMPORTANT: Only IN binding is supported.
