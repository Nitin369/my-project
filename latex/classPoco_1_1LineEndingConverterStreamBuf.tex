\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This stream buffer performs line ending conversion on text streams. The converter can convert from and to the Unix (LF), Mac (CR) and D\+O\+S/\+Windows/\+Network (C\+F-\/\+LF) endings.

\mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Any}{Any}} newline sequence in the source will be replaced by the target newline sequence.
