\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This is the streambuf class used by \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1InflatingInputStream}{Inflating\+Input\+Stream}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1InflatingOutputStream}{Inflating\+Output\+Stream}}. The actual work is delegated to zlib (see \href{http://zlib.net}{\texttt{ http\+://zlib.\+net}}). Both zlib (deflate) streams and gzip streams are supported. Output streams should always call \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1InflatingStreamBuf_a407f572893f12db2e246db629a82e3d3}{close()}} to ensure proper completion of decompression.
