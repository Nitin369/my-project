The Net\+S\+SL will be initialized automatically, through \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Crypto_1_1OpenSSLInitializer}{Poco\+::\+Crypto\+::\+Open\+S\+S\+L\+Initializer}} instances or similar mechanisms when creating \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Net_1_1Context}{Context}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Net_1_1SSLManager}{S\+S\+L\+Manager}} instances. Can be called multiple times; however, for every call to \mbox{\hyperlink{namespacePoco_1_1Net_a60f251cbb4aeeb12166f8ed195720533}{initialize\+S\+S\+L()}}, a matching call to \mbox{\hyperlink{namespacePoco_1_1Net_ae19a5a8fb56d82578ff76a226ac00772}{uninitialize\+S\+S\+L()}} must be performed.
