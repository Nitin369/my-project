\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This is the streambuf class used by \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1DeflatingInputStream}{Deflating\+Input\+Stream}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1DeflatingOutputStream}{Deflating\+Output\+Stream}}. The actual work is delegated to zlib (see \href{http://zlib.net}{\texttt{ http\+://zlib.\+net}}). Both zlib (deflate) streams and gzip streams are supported. Output streams should always call \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1DeflatingStreamBuf_a537ef6c969d9f141d3239b14fa862097}{close()}} to ensure proper completion of compression. A compression level (0 to 9) can be specified in the constructor.
