\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This interface represents a notation declared in the D\+TD. A notation either declares, by name, the format of an unparsed entity (see section 4.\+7 of the \mbox{\hyperlink{namespacePoco_1_1XML}{X\+ML}} 1.\+0 specification \href{http://www.w3.org/TR/2004/REC-xml-20040204/}{\texttt{ http\+://www.\+w3.\+org/\+T\+R/2004/\+R\+E\+C-\/xml-\/20040204/}}), or is used for formal declaration of processing instruction targets (see section 2.\+6 of the \mbox{\hyperlink{namespacePoco_1_1XML}{X\+ML}} 1.\+0 specification). The node\+Name attribute inherited from \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1XML_1_1Node}{Node}} is set to the declared name of the notation.

The D\+OM Level 1 does not support editing \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1XML_1_1Notation}{Notation}} nodes; they are therefore readonly.

A \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1XML_1_1Notation}{Notation}} node does not have any parent.
