For details, see the standard library implementation of std\+::vector$<$bool$>$ or S. Meyers\+: \char`\"{}\+Effective S\+T\+L\char`\"{} (Copyright Addison-\/\+Wesley 2001), Item 18\+: \char`\"{}\+Avoid using vector$<$bool$>$.\char`\"{}

The workaround employed here is using std\+::deque$<$bool$>$ as an internal replacement container.

I\+M\+P\+O\+R\+T\+A\+NT\+: Only IN binding is supported.
