\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Task}{Task}} is a subclass of \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Runnable}{Runnable}} that has a name and supports progress reporting and cancellation.

A \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1TaskManager}{Task\+Manager}} object can be used to take care of the lifecycle of a \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Task}{Task}}.
