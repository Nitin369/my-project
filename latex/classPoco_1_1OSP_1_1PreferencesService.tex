\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1PreferencesService}{Preferences\+Service}} provides an easy way for a bundle or service to retrieve and store configuration information. It also gives a bundle or service read-\/only access to the global application configuration.

The service name of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1PreferencesService}{Preferences\+Service}} is \char`\"{}osp.\+core.\+preferences\char`\"{}.
