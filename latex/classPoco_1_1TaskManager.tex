\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1TaskManager}{Task\+Manager}} manages a collection of tasks and monitors their lifetime.

A \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1TaskManager}{Task\+Manager}} has a built-\/in \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1NotificationCenter}{Notification\+Center}} that is used to send out notifications on task progress and task states. See the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1TaskNotification}{Task\+Notification}} class and its subclasses for the various events that result in a notification. To keep the number of notifications small, a \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1TaskProgressNotification}{Task\+Progress\+Notification}} will only be sent out once in 100 milliseconds.
