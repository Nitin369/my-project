\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This ostream encodes all data written to it in quoted-\/printable encoding (see R\+FC 2045) and forwards it to a connected ostream. Always call \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Net_1_1QuotedPrintableEncoderIOS_ac994ff2c79fd175ada9b6833a5a53f49}{close()}} when done writing data, to ensure proper completion of the encoding operation.
