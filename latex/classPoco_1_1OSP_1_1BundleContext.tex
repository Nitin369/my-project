\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1BundleContext}{Bundle\+Context}} gives a \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1BundleActivator}{Bundle\+Activator}} access to the bundle\textquotesingle{}s runtime environment. Via the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1BundleContext}{Bundle\+Context}}, the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1BundleActivator}{Bundle\+Activator}} can obtain a pointer to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1Bundle}{Bundle}} object, to system-\/wide configuration properties and to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1ServiceRegistry}{Service\+Registry}}. The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1BundleContext}{Bundle\+Context}} can also be used to find other bundles in the system.
