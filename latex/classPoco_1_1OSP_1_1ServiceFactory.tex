\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1ServiceFactory}{Service\+Factory}} is a special \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1Service}{Service}} that, as its name implies, acts as a factory for \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1Service}{Service}} objects.

If a subclass of \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1ServiceFactory}{Service\+Factory}} is registered as a service, the factory will be used to create a new instance of the service whenever the service is requested from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1ServiceRegistry}{Service\+Registry}}.
