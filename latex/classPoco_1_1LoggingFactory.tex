\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
An extensible factory for channels and formatters. \begin{DoxyVerb}The following channel classes are pre-registered:
  - AsyncChannel
  - ConsoleChannel
  - EventLogChannel (Windows platforms only)
  - FileChannel
  - FormattingChannel
  - NullChannel
  - OpcomChannel (OpenVMS only)
  - SplitterChannel
  - SyslogChannel (Unix platforms only)

The following formatter classes are pre-registered:
  - PatternFormatter 
\end{DoxyVerb}
