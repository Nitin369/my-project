\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This ostream computes a digest of all the data passing through it, using a \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1DigestEngine}{Digest\+Engine}}. To ensure that all data has been incorporated into the digest, call \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1DigestOutputStream_a9bb59fa923283af82fda95db24c62b91}{close()}} or flush() before you obtain the digest from the digest engine.
