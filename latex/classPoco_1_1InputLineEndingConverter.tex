\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1InputLineEndingConverter}{Input\+Line\+Ending\+Converter}} performs line ending conversion on text input streams. The converter can convert from and to the Unix (LF), Mac (CR) and D\+O\+S/\+Windows/\+Network (C\+F-\/\+LF) endings.

\mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1Any}{Any}} newline sequence in the source will be replaced by the target newline sequence.
