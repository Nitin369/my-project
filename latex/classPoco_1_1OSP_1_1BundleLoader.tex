\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1BundleLoader}{Bundle\+Loader}} is used internally by the framework to manage \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1Bundle}{Bundle}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1BundleContext}{Bundle\+Context}} objects, and to load shared libraries containing \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoco_1_1OSP_1_1BundleActivator}{Bundle\+Activator}} classes.
