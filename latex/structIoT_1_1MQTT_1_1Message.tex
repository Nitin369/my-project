{\footnotesize\ttfamily bool Io\+T\+::\+M\+Q\+T\+T\+::\+Message\+::retained}

The quality of service (QoS) assigned to the message. There are three levels of QoS\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item 0\+: Fire and forget -\/ the message may not be delivered.
\item 1\+: At least once -\/ the message will be delivered, but may be delivered more than once in some circumstances.
\item 2\+: Once and one only -\/ the message will be delivered exactly once. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
